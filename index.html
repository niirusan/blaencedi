Our Farm

Blaen Cedi provides all the mod-cons to allow you to relax in the beautiful Welsh country side, in comfort. The rooms have been carefully furnished and have a unique luxurious style that makes them feel quite special and is definitely memorable. Blaen Cedi is 4 star graded by Visit Wales.


The grounds are accessed through a secure electric gate and the cottages are found hidden away at the end of a short, green, leafy lane.


Relax, destress and step into the ambiance of Blaen Cedi. Experience feelings of peace, tranquility, comfort and luxury. Soak up the ambiance of rural seclusion.


Our family, with young children, lives on the property. We will be there to answer queries, provide support and guidance on local attractions and activities during your stay. We are outdoor people and love mini adventures and will be happy to share.
